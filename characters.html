Meet Our Characters

The inspiring heroes driving sustainable development across Uganda

SDG 1: No Poverty

Nakato

A visionary farmer from Kabale who revolutionized local agriculture through sustainable practices. Her microfinance initiative has helped over 200 women start their own businesses.

Connected to: Auma (Water Project), Sarah (Solar Engineering)
SDG 13: Climate Action

Okello

Young environmental activist leading the Budongo Forest conservation efforts. His youth club has planted over 10,000 trees and created innovative solutions for human-wildlife conflict.

Connected to: Dr. Namugga (Wildlife Research), Mutesi (Urban Planning)
SDG 5 & 7: Gender Equality & Clean Energy

Sarah

Pioneer female solar engineer breaking gender barriers in rural electrification. She's trained 50 other women in solar technology and runs a successful renewable energy enterprise.

Connected to: Nakato (Agriculture), Esther (Technology Education)
SDG 4: Quality Education

Daniel

Refugee teacher in Bidi Bidi settlement who developed a multilingual education program. His mobile library initiative reaches children across multiple settlements.

Connected to: Grace (Mental Health), Ibrahim (Peace Building)
SDG 6: Clean Water

Auma

Community water engineer in Gulu leading innovations in sustainable water access. Her team has developed low-cost water filtration systems and trains communities in maintenance.

Connected to: Dr. Kiiza (Health), Nakato (Agriculture)
SDG 11: Sustainable Cities

Mutesi

Urban planner transforming Kampala's neighborhoods through community-led development. Her projects focus on green spaces, sustainable transport, and inclusive housing.

Connected to: Okello (Environment), James (Innovation)
SDG 3: Good Health

Dr. Kiiza

Rural healthcare innovator who created a mobile clinic network. Her telemedicine program connects remote villages with specialist doctors in urban centers.

Connected to: Auma (Water), Grace (Mental Health)
SDG 9: Industry & Innovation

James

Tech entrepreneur developing affordable agricultural technologies. His solar-powered cold storage solution helps farmers reduce post-harvest losses.

Connected to: Nakato (Agriculture), Sarah (Solar Energy)
SDG 3: Mental Health

Grace

Mental health advocate working with refugee communities. She pioneered art therapy programs and trains community mental health workers.

Connected to: Daniel (Education), Dr. Kiiza (Healthcare)
SDG 16: Peace & Justice

Ibrahim

Former refugee turned peace-building facilitator. He runs conflict resolution workshops and youth leadership programs across settlement communities.

Connected to: Daniel (Education), Grace (Mental Health)
SDG 8: Decent Work

Esther

Digital skills trainer empowering youth with technology education. Her coding bootcamps have helped hundreds of young people secure remote work opportunities.

Connected to: Sarah (Technology), James (Innovation)
SDG 15: Life on Land

Dr. Namugga

Wildlife researcher studying human-wildlife coexistence. Her community-based conservation approach has become a model for protected areas across Uganda.

Connected to: Okello (Environment), Mutesi (Urban Planning)
