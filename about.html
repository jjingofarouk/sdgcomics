About Uganda SDG Storytelling

Bringing Uganda's sustainable development journey to life through diverse storytelling mediums that educate, inspire, and drive action.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To harness the power of visual storytelling to make the Sustainable Development Goals accessible, relatable, and actionable for all Ugandans, while preserving and celebrating our rich cultural heritage.

Vision

A Uganda where every citizen understands, embraces, and actively contributes to achieving the Sustainable Development Goals through locally-driven solutions and community engagement.

Values

  • Cultural Authenticity
  • Community Empowerment
  • Inclusive Storytelling
  • Educational Innovation
  • Sustainable Impact

Our Approach to Social Change

Visual Storytelling

Leveraging diverse creative formats like comics, illustrations, and infographics to simplify complex SDG concepts into engaging and relatable stories.

Community Engagement

Collaborating with local communities to gather authentic stories, ensure cultural relevance, and co-create narratives that inspire collective action.

Educational Integration

Designing versatile educational resources, including workshops, digital content, and guides, to enhance learning and awareness in schools and organizations.
