Communities Represented

The Seeds of Change (SDG 1: No Poverty)

Follow Nakato, a determined farmer in Kabale, as she transforms her community through innovative agricultural practices and microfinance initiatives. Her story shows how local solutions can break the cycle of poverty.

Guardians of the Forest (SDG 13: Climate Action)

Join Okello and his youth environmental club in Budongo Forest as they combat deforestation, protect endangered chimpanzees, and implement community-based climate solutions.

Breaking Barriers (SDG 5: Gender Equality)

Experience Sarah's inspiring journey as she challenges traditional norms to become her village's first female solar engineer, empowering other women along the way.

Dreams Beyond Borders (SDG 4: Quality Education)

Follow the story of Daniel, a refugee teacher in Bidi Bidi settlement, who creates innovative learning solutions for children from diverse backgrounds.

The Water Keepers (SDG 6: Clean Water)

Meet Auma and her team of community water engineers in Gulu as they develop sustainable solutions for clean water access and sanitation.

Urban Dreams (SDG 11: Sustainable Cities)

Experience Kampala's transformation through the eyes of Mutesi, an urban planner working with local communities to create sustainable, inclusive neighborhoods.
